Michael Thompson

Age: 38 years old

Marital Status: Married

Location: Chicago, IL

Scenario

Michael is a parent juggling cooking dinner and managing kids’ needs after picking them up from the after-school program.

Expectations

  • Quick meal preparation
  • Quality time with kids
  • Easy cleanup

Key Activities

  1. Picks up kids from the after-school program.
  2. Improvises a quick dinner based on what’s in the fridge/freezer.
  3. Manages distractions from kids asking to play or expressing hunger.
  4. Prepares and serves the meal while managing kids’ impatience.
  5. Cleans up after dinner and transitions kids to bedtime routine.

Emotional Responses

  • Frustration: At not being able to cook as quickly as he’d like.
  • Guilt: For not spending enough time with his kids while preparing dinner.
  • Stress: From balancing cooking, managing kids’ needs, and staying on schedule.
  • Happiness: When sitting down for dinner as a family, even if the time is short.

Tools/Technologies

  • Smartphone for recipes and time-checking.
  • Kitchen appliances (stove, microwave, dishwasher).

Pain Points

  • Lack of time to prepare meals and engage with kids simultaneously.
  • Repetitive meal options due to time and mental energy constraints.
  • Mess and cleanup after dinner, adding to workload.
